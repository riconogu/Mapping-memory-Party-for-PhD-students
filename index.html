PRE-COVID PARTY SCENE FOR GRADUATE STUDENTS IN LANSING/EAST LANSING METROPOLITAN AREA

Friendship is critical for a meaningful scholarship, especially for PhD students who are prone to be isolated. Partying is one of the most straightforward methods to build new friendship, at least before COVID-19. We create a map for MSU Ph.D. students looking to find a place where friendship can grow with the help of good conversations, or beers, or dancing, or all of those things at the same time.
